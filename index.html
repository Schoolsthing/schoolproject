Overview

  • Mexico chosen for food, warm climate, and vibrant culture
  • Popular for beaches, ruins, and cuisine
  • Population about 130 million; capital is Mexico City

Language & Currency

  • Official language is Spanish
  • Currency used: Mexican peso
  • Majority follow Roman Catholicism

Food Insecurity

  • 30–60% affected depending on region
  • Rural areas struggle more with affordable nutritious food
  • Impacts health and cultural food traditions

Geography & Climate

  • Ideal travel time: November, warm and dry
  • Varied landscapes: deserts, jungles, mountains
  • Coastal areas have seafood; inland relies on corn, beans, beef

Food & Culture

  • Famous dishes: tacos, mole, tamales, chiles en nogada, pozole
  • Dining: 3 meals/day, lunch is biggest
  • Día de Muertos celebrated Nov 1 & 2 with special foods
